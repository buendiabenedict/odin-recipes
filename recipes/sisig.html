Sisig

Description

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from pork jowl and ears, pork belly, and chicken liver, which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers. It originates from the Pampanga region in Luzon. Sisig is a staple of Kapampangan cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Mukha ng baboy (nguso at tainga)
  • Atay ng manok
  • Sibuyas
  • Siling labuyo
  • Siling haba
  • Calamansi
  • Toyo
  • Paminta
  • Asin
  • Mantikilya o Margarine
  • Itlog (optional)
  • Mayonnaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Laga: Pakuluan ang mukha at tainga ng baboy hanggang lumambot, saka patuyuin.
  2. Ihaw: Ihawin ang karne at atay hanggang maging malutong at tostado.
  3. Tadtad: Hiwain ang karne at atay nang maliliit at pino.
  4. Igisa: Tunawin ang mantikilya, igisa ang sibuyas, at ihalo ang tinadtad na karne.
  5. Timpla: Ihalo ang toyo, calamansi, sili, asin, at paminta.
  6. Serve: Ilagay sa mainit na sizzling plate at ihalo ang itlog o mayonnaise kung gusto.
